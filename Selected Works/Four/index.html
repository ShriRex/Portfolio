Sun Temple Konark - Brand Identity

Designed a brand identity for the World Heritage Site, Sun Temple in Konark, Odisha, India. Sun Temple Konark is a 13th century CE temple dedicated to Hindu Sun God, Surya. The Temple is a monumental representation of Surya's Chariot and exemplifies the Kalinga architecture native to Odisha. The scale, refinement, perfect form and conception represent strength and stability associated with the Ganga Empire it was built under. I visited this Temple in 2014. I still vividly remember how mesmerizing the Temple looked. The place felt ethereal and so other world-like. I wanted to create a system that captured the essence of the place.
